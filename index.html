How to Scan an Invoice

  1. Tap "Scan Invoice" from the main dashboard
  2. Position the invoice fully within the camera frame
  3. Ensure good lighting and hold device steady
  4. Tap capture when invoice is clear and readable
  5. Review extracted information for accuracy
  6. Edit any mistakes - this helps the AI learn!
  7. Save the invoice to add items to your inventory

Tips for Best Results

üí° Good Lighting

üîßTroubleshooting

Problem: Invoice scanning is not accurate
Solution: Ensure good lighting, hold device steady, keep entire invoice visible, and edit mistakes to help AI learn.
Problem: Cannot edit users or access certain features
Solution: Check your user role - only Administrators can manage users. Contact your system administrator for role changes.
Problem: Email sending fails
Solution: Check internet connection, verify email app configuration, and confirm recipient addresses in Email Setup.
Problem: App crashes or runs slowly
Solution: Close and restart the app, restart device if needed, ensure adequate storage space, update iOS.
Problem: Missing invoices or inventory data
Solution: Verify correct user login, check different business profiles, ensure device backups are current.
